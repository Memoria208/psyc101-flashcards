# PSYC 101 Flashcards

An interactive flashcard study app for **PSYC 101: Introduction to Psychology** 

---

## What it does

- **Flip cards** — tap or click any card to reveal the definition
- **Track progress** — mark cards as "Know it" or "Still learning" and watch a progress bar fill
- **Filter by topic** — chips at the top let you focus on one category (e.g. Research Methods, History, Ethics)
- **Chapter selector** — choose one chapter, several, or all of them before you start
- **Finals mode** — select all available chapters to study the whole semester at once
- **Dark mode** — automatically follows your system preference
- **No login, no account** — just open the page and study

---

## How to use it

1. **Open the app** → [Memoria208.github.io/psyc101-flashcards](https://Memoria208.github.io/psyc101-flashcards)
2. On the home screen, **click the chapters** you want to study (they highlight green when selected)
3. Hit **Start flashcards**
4. **Tap a card** to flip it and see the definition
5. Mark it **Know it ✓** or **Still learning ↺**
6. Use the **filter chips** to drill into a specific topic within your session
7. When you finish the deck, hit **Study again** to reshuffle, or **Choose chapters** to go back
